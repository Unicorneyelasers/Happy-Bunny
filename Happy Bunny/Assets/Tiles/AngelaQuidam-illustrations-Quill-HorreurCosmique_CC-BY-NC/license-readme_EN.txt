Inside this file you will find scans and illustrations (JPG format, black and white)
created for the French translation of the "cosmic horror" Quill supplement
including the Shadow & Ink campaign and the scenarios The Horror on the Hill & The Haunting of Pettigrew Manor
writen by Scott Malthouse (https://www.trollishdelver.com). 
– French version december 2019, by angela quidam for the association La Caravelle (https://fr.tipeee.com/la-caravelle)


Illustrations are made with Chinese ink and fine point pen.
